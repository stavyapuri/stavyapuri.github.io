Research Interests

Quantum Batteries

Investigating Floquet-driven quantum batteries with long-range interactions, demonstrating super-extensive scaling of maximum power.

Quantum Cryptography

Analyzing security proofs for Quantum Key Distribution protocols using information-theoretic definitions and entropic uncertainty principles.

Quantum Sensing

Environmental scan on quantum sensors based on electron spin resonance, with focus on NV-Diamond sensors and their applications.

Open Quantum Systems

Studying many-body spin chain models and quantum dynamics using Liouville Von-Neumann evolution and Lindblad equations.
